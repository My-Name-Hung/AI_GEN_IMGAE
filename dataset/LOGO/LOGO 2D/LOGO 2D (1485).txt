Mô tả Hình ảnh:

- Bảng màu: Đen, trắng và cam

Visual Description:
This logo depicts a bearded man with classic hairstyle inside a circular frame decorated with orange dots. Below is an orange banner ribbon containing the brand name "FINEZ" and the tagline "The Revered Man".

Design Elements:
- Shape: Circular emblem with curved banner ribbon below
- Typography: Bold uppercase letters in vintage style for the brand name
- Color Palette: Black, white, and orange
- Background: Black background
- Style: Vintage, retro, barbershop
- Mood: Masculine, classic, strong
