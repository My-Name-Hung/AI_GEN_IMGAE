Visual Description:
The logo features a circular design with a fresh orange and orange slice at the top, surrounded by vibrant green leaves. The center displays a cream-colored ribbon banner with "NATURAL" in bold letters, and below shows "ORANGE" with the slogan "100% ORGANIC & FRESH".

Design Elements:
- Shape: Circular with horizontal ribbon banner across the middle
- Typography: Bold uppercase font with vintage styling
- Color Palette: Green, orange, yellow, cream
- Background: White/transparent background
- Style: Vintage, natural, organic
- Mood: Fresh, natural, quality-focused
