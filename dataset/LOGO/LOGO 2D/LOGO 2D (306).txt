- Bảng màu: Đen, xanh lá cây neon, vàng cam
- Nền: Trắng, sạch sẽ

- Tông điệu: Năng động, âm nhạc, công nghệ

Visual Description:
The logo features a black circular icon with concentric curved lines in green and orange, resembling a spinning vinyl record. Below is the brand name "Spinnify" in bold black lettering with "VINYL RECORDS STORE" in smaller text underneath.

Design Elements:
- Shape: Circular with concentric curves, mimicking a vinyl record
- Typography: Bold sans-serif for main name and thin capitals for tagline
- Color Palette: Black, neon green, orange
- Background: Clean white
- Style: Modern, minimalist, retro-tech
- Mood: Dynamic, musical, technological
