Visual Description:
The logo features a circular design in light pink with a cupcake illustration at the center, topped with white frosting and a red cherry. The text "BÁNH" appears at the top, "NGON" at the bottom, and "TỪ 2015" on either side of the cupcake.

Design Elements:
- Shape: Circular with central cupcake icon
- Typography: Bold uppercase letters, simple and readable style
- Color Palette: Light pink, red-orange, white
- Background: Light pink circular background
- Style: Simple, friendly, retro aesthetic
- Mood: Sweet, warm, appetizing
