- Bảng màu: Xanh lá cây, đen, trắng, tím
- Nền: Tím với họa tiết hình học đối xứng
- Phong cách: Gaming, esports, hiện đại, chuyên nghiệp
- Tông điệu: Mạnh mẽ, bí ẩn, công nghệ cao

Visual Description:
The logo features a triangular shield shape in bright green with white border, containing black "ABC" text and an illustration of a character wearing a gas mask with purple-tinted goggles. Below the shield is Vietnamese text on a purple geometric patterned background.

- Shape: Triangular shield with downward-pointing apex
- Typography: Bold, modern font for "ABC" and smaller font for tagline
- Color Palette: Bright green, black, white, purple
- Background: Purple with symmetrical geometric patterns
- Style: Gaming, esports, modern, professional
- Mood: Strong, mysterious, high-tech
