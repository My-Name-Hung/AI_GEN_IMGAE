Visual Description:
The logo features a navy blue anchor symbol at the top, below which is the text "QUỸ KHUYẾN HỌC" in red uppercase letters. At the bottom is the tagline "Vì một tương lai tốt hơn" in light gray italics, accompanied by a decorative horizontal line.

Design Elements:
- Shape: Simple anchor symbol with two curved arms
- Typography: Sans-serif uppercase font for main text, italics for tagline
- Color Palette: Navy blue, red, light gray
- Background: White background
- Style: Simple, formal, academic
- Mood: Serious, trustworthy, educational
