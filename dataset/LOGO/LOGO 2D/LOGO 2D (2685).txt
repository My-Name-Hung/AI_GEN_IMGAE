- Bảng màu: Đen trắng (đơn sắc)
- Nền: Nền trắng trong suốt
- Phong cách: Hàng hải, cổ điển, vintage, biểu tượng

Visual Description:
The logo features a circular design with an anchor at the center, surrounded by dots and serrated edges forming a ring. Below is the text "SEAWORTHY" in capital letters with the tagline "THE SOUL SINGS ONLY IN THE SEA".

Design Elements:
- Shape: Circular badge with central anchor and serrated border details
- Typography: Bold traditional serif capitals
- Color Palette: Black and white (monochrome)
- Background: Clear white background
- Style: Nautical, classic, vintage, emblematic
- Mood: Strong, authoritative, maritime, adventurous
