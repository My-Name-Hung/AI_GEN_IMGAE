- Bảng màu: Xanh lá cây, trắng, xanh dương nhạt

Visual Description:
This is an advertising poster for INAX NANO protective paint products. The main image shows a modern house covered by a transparent protective shield with glowing effects, with INAX paint cans positioned at the bottom corner against a blue sky background.

Design Elements:
- Shape: Text-based logo with circular green leaf symbol
- Typography: Modern sans-serif, bold "INAX" with smaller "NANO"
- Color Palette: Green, white, light blue
- Background: White in logo, blue sky in overall poster
- Style: Minimalist, modern, high-tech
- Mood: Professional, trustworthy, eco-friendly
